Our Partners

At Excelsia Health Travels, we believe in the power of collaboration to deliver world-class medical and birth tourism services. Our success is built on strong partnerships with internationally accredited hospitals, healthcare providers, travel experts, and service professionals. Together, we ensure that our clients receive the highest standard of care, comfort, and support throughout their medical or birth tourism journey.

Why Partner with Excelsia Health Travels?

We are Nigeria’s leading medical and birth tourism service provider, offering seamless, safe, and rewarding experiences for clients seeking healthcare or maternity services abroad. By partnering with us, you join a trusted network of global healthcare providers, travel experts, and service professionals dedicated to transforming lives.

Benefits of Partnering with Us

  • Access to a Growing Market: As the demand for medical and birth tourism continues to grow, partnering with Excelsia Health Travels gives you access to a diverse and expanding client base from Nigeria and across Africa.
  • Trusted Expertise: With years of experience in medical and birth tourism, we have built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and professionalism. Our partners benefit from our expertise in coordinating complex healthcare journeys.
  • Global Network: Join a network of internationally accredited hospitals, renowned specialists, and trusted service providers across top destinations, including the United States, Canada, India, Turkey, UAE, and more.
  • Seamless Collaboration: We handle all aspects of the client journey, from visa assistance and travel arrangements to hospital coordination and post-treatment care, ensuring a smooth process for both clients and partners.
  • Enhanced Visibility: As a partner, your services will be featured on our platform, giving you increased visibility and credibility among our clients and affiliates.

Our Partners Include

We collaborate with leading hospitals, clinics, and service providers worldwide to ensure our clients receive the best care and support. Our partners include:

1. Internationally Accredited Hospitals and Clinics

We collaborate with leading hospitals and clinics worldwide, ensuring our clients receive top-quality care. Our hospital partners are carefully selected based on their accreditation, reputation, and expertise in various medical specialties, including:

  • Cardiac care
  • Orthopedic surgery
  • Fertility treatments
  • Oncology
  • Cosmetic surgery
  • Maternity care
2. Renowned Medical Specialists

Our network includes highly skilled and board-certified doctors, surgeons, and specialists who are leaders in their fields. We connect our clients with experts who provide personalized care and innovative treatments.

3. Travel and Accommodation Providers

We work with trusted airlines, hotels, and serviced apartments to provide our clients with comfortable and convenient travel and accommodation options. Our travel partners ensure a seamless experience, from airport pickups to local transportation.

4. Visa and Legal Experts

Navigating visa applications and legal requirements can be challenging. That’s why we partner with visa consultants and legal experts who specialize in medical and birth tourism, ensuring our clients have the necessary documentation for a smooth journey.

5. Insurance Providers

We collaborate with international and local insurance companies to help clients access coverage for medical treatments, maternity care, and travel emergencies.

6. Affiliates and Referrers

Our affiliate program includes healthcare professionals, travel agents, and influencers who help us reach more clients. Affiliates earn commissions for every successful referral, creating a mutually beneficial relationship.

Become a Partner

Are you a hospital, healthcare provider, travel expert, or service professional looking to expand your reach and impact? Join our network of trusted partners and help us deliver life-changing healthcare and birth tourism experiences.

What We Look for in Our Partners
  • Accreditation and Expertise: We prioritize partners with international certifications (e.g., JCI, NABH) and a proven track record of excellence.
  • Commitment to Quality: Our partners must share our dedication to providing safe, ethical, and high-quality services.
  • Client-Centric Approach: We value partners who prioritize client satisfaction and go the extra mile to ensure a positive experience.
  • Transparency: We work with partners who provide clear, upfront pricing and maintain open communication.
How to Partner with Us
  1. Submit Your Application: Fill out our partnership application form on our website or contact us via email at travels@excelsiahealth.net. Provide details about your services, certifications, and areas of expertise.
  2. Evaluation and Onboarding: Our team will review your application and conduct a thorough evaluation to ensure alignment with our standards. Once approved, we’ll onboard you into our network.
  3. Collaborate and Grow: Start collaborating with us to provide exceptional medical and birth tourism services. Together, we’ll help clients achieve their healthcare and maternity goals.

Our Commitment to Partners

At Excelsia Health Travels, we value our partnerships and are committed to fostering long-term, mutually beneficial relationships. We provide our partners with:

  • Dedicated Support: A dedicated account manager to assist with coordination and communication.
  • Marketing Opportunities: Exposure through our website, social media, and client referrals.
  • Continuous Collaboration: Regular updates, feedback, and opportunities to enhance our joint services.
