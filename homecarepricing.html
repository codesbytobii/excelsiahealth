Premium Pricing Plan

At Homecare Nigeria, we pride ourselves on delivering the highest quality of care with personalised services tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. Our team of highly trained carers ensures that you or your loved ones receive exceptional and compassionate care. Below is a reflection of our premium pricing structure, designed to guarantee top-tier service and peace of mind.

Hourly Care Services

Best for: Short-term support, daily assistance, or respite care.

  • Basic Care (Companionship, personal care, light household tasks): From ₦8,000 per hour
  • Specialised Care (Dementia care, stroke care, neurological conditions): From ₦10,000 per hour
  • Night Care (Sleep-in care): From ₦20,000 per night

Live-in Care

Best for: Continuous 24-hour care and support in the comfort of your own home.

  • Basic Live-in Care: From ₦70,000 per week
  • Specialised Live-in Care (Alzheimer’s, Parkinson’s, palliative care): From ₦100,000 per week
  • Complex Live-in Care (For advanced medical needs like cancer, spinal injury): From ₦150,000 per week

24-Hour Care

Best for: Round-the-clock care with rotating carers for uninterrupted support.

  • Standard 24-Hour Care: From ₦120,000 per week
  • Specialised 24-Hour Care (Dementia, Parkinson’s, neurological conditions): From ₦150,000 per week

Respite Care

Best for: Temporary relief for primary caregivers.

  • Short-Term Respite (up to 4 hours per day): From ₦25,000 per day
  • Extended Respite Care (24-hour support): From ₦100,000 per day

Condition-led Care

Best for: Chronic conditions or complex medical needs requiring specialist support.

  • Diabetes Care: From ₦10,000 per hour
  • Parkinson’s Care: From ₦10,000 per hour
  • Palliative Care: From ₦12,000 per hour

Premium Care Packages

At Homecare Nigeria, we understand that every individual’s needs are unique. To make it easier for you or your loved ones to receive the highest standard of care, we’ve developed a range of care packages tailored to different levels of service and support. Each package is designed to ensure the well-being, comfort, and independence of our clients while offering flexibility to match specific care requirements.

Basic Package

Ideal for: Individuals requiring assistance with everyday tasks and basic care needs.

Price: ₦80,000 per week

Included Services:

  • 4 hours of care per day
  • Assistance with personal care (bathing, dressing, grooming)
  • Light household tasks (cleaning, laundry, tidying)
  • Medication reminders and basic medication management
  • Meal preparation and assistance with feeding
  • Daily companionship and emotional support
  • Regular welfare checks
  • Assistance with mobility and transfers

Extra Benefits:

  • Complimentary health assessment and care planning
  • Access to emergency support
  • 10% discount on additional hours of care beyond the package

Silver Package

Ideal for: Individuals requiring more hands-on care, including support for medical conditions like diabetes or arthritis.

Price: ₦110,000 per week

Included Services:

  • 8 hours of care per day
  • All Basic Package services
  • Specialised care for chronic conditions (diabetes, arthritis)
  • Mobility support and light physical exercises
  • In-home physical therapy coordination (in collaboration with healthcare professionals)
  • Full medication management (administering and monitoring)
  • Accompanying for medical appointments (2 trips per week)
  • Regular progress reports and health updates for family members

Extra Benefits:

  • Free quarterly health assessments
  • Priority access to our 24/7 helpline
  • 15% discount on additional hours of care

Gold Package

Ideal for: Individuals with complex health needs requiring specialised care, including conditions like dementia, Parkinson’s, or post-operative recovery.

Price: ₦170,000 per week

Included Services:

  • 12 hours of care per day
  • All Silver Package services
  • Dementia, Parkinson’s, and palliative care
  • Comprehensive post-surgical care (e.g., wound care, nephrostomy/stoma management)
  • Nutritional planning and specialised meal preparation
  • Advanced pain management and monitoring of vital signs
  • Emotional support for family members, including counselling sessions
  • Accompanying for up to 4 medical appointments per week
  • Coordination with multiple healthcare providers

Extra Benefits:

  • Dedicated care coordinator for seamless care management
  • 20% discount on all additional services (physiotherapy, counselling, etc.)
  • Access to mental health and wellness programs

Platinum Package

Ideal for: Individuals requiring 24/7 care and support with advanced medical conditions, or those who want the most comprehensive care plan available.

Price: ₦250,000 per week

Included Services:

  • 24/7 care with rotating carers for continuous support
  • All Gold Package services
  • Live-in care with personalised attention around the clock
  • Full pain management, medication administration, and mobility assistance

  • Personalised rehabilitation and physiotherapy support
  • Emotional and social support, including companionship and lifestyle activities
  • Full assistance with feeding, toileting, and all personal care tasks
  • Weekly visits by a healthcare professional (doctor/nurse)
  • Medical transportation trips per week

Extra Benefits:

  • Personal health concierge to manage all healthcare needs
  • Priority support to specialist consultations (oncology, neurology, etc.)
  • Free family support program, including mental health resources and wellness coaching
  • Unlimited access to our 24/7 emergency support line

Payment Terms & Flexibility

At Homecare Nigeria, we are committed to offering transparent and flexible payment options to ensure that the premium care services you receive fit seamlessly into your financial planning. We aim to make payment stress-free, providing multiple options to suit different needs.

Flexible Payment Options:

  • Weekly Billing: Payments can be made at the start of each week, ensuring flexibility for short-term care or those preferring more frequent billing cycles.
  • Monthly Billing: For clients opting for long-term care plans, monthly billing is available for added convenience. Payments are due at the start of each month for services rendered.
  • Quarterly Payments: For those requiring consistent care over extended periods, we offer quarterly billing at a discounted rate. This option helps plan finances for long-term care, allowing for budgeting ease.

Payment Methods:

  • Bank Transfer: Direct bank transfer to our company account is available and preferred for secure, straightforward payments.
  • Credit/Debit Cards: We accept all major credit and debit cards. Payments can be set up for recurring billing to avoid manual payments.
  • Online Payment Gateway: Clients can make payments through our secure online payment platform, accessible via our website.
  • Standing Orders: For ongoing care plans, you may set up a standing order with your bank to ensure automatic payments are made on time.
  • Mobile Money Payments: For added convenience, we also support mobile money transfers for clients who prefer using mobile banking apps or wallets.

Deposit Requirements:

  • Initial Deposit: For live-in care or long-term care packages, a 20% deposit is required at the time of booking to secure your care team and ensure seamless service. This deposit will be credited toward your first payment.
  • Refundable Security Deposit: For 24/7 or live-in care, a one-time refundable security deposit is required to cover any unexpected medical supplies or emergency care services. This will be refunded at the end of the service contract if unused.

Discounts and Special Offers:

  • Prepayment Discounts: Clients who prepay for 3 months or more in advance receive a 5% discount on their total care package.
  • Family Discount: If multiple family members are receiving care services from us, we offer a 10% discount on the second family member’s care plan.
  • Referral Bonus: Existing clients can receive ₦50,000 off their next bill when referring a new client who signs up for one of our care packages.

Flexible Adjustments:

  • Care Plan Adjustments: We understand that care needs can change over time. You may adjust your care plan or upgrade/downgrade your package at any time with 7 days' notice. Any changes in the care plan will be reflected in the next billing cycle.
  • Pause or Cancel Services: Clients are free to pause or cancel their care plans with 2 weeks' notice for monthly billing plans and 1 week’s notice for weekly plans. No cancellation fees apply.

Late Payment Policy:

  • Grace Period: We offer a 5-day grace period for payments before any late fees are applied. After the grace period, a 5% late fee may be added to the outstanding balance.
  • Payment Plan for Overdue Balances: If you're unable to make a payment on time, we can work with you to set up a payment plan for any overdue balances, ensuring that your care services remain uninterrupted.

Insurance & Coverage Options:

  • Health Insurance: If you have health insurance that covers home care services, we can work with your provider to process claims and help minimise out-of-pocket costs.
  • Employer Coverage: Some employers offer subsidies for home care services. We can provide the necessary documentation to assist in employer-based coverage applications.
  • Government Support Programmes: If eligible, we can help guide you through applying for any available government assistance programs to offset the cost of care.

Why Choose Our Payment Options?

  • Flexibility: Choose between weekly, monthly, or quarterly billing options to match your financial planning.
  • Secure Payments: Multiple secure payment methods ensure your transactions are safe and efficient.
  • Discounts and Offers: Take advantage of early payment discounts and family packages to save more.
  • Customisation: Adjust your care plan or payment schedule at any time without penalties.
  • Ease of Use: Recurring payments and online options simplify the billing process, so you never miss a payment.
