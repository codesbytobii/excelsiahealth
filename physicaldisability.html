Physical Disabilities Care Services in Nigeria

Living with a physical disability should never limit your ability to enjoy a rich and fulfilling life. At Homecare Nigeria, we are dedicated to providing personalized home care services that empower individuals with physical disabilities, whether newly diagnosed or managing their condition for a long time. Our goal is to promote independence, dignity, and a high quality of life through tailored care solutions.

Personalised Care for Disabled Adults

We believe in a collaborative approach, working closely with individuals and their families to understand their unique needs, preferences, and goals. Whether you're striving to achieve long-held aspirations or adjusting to a new diagnosis, we’re here to support you every step of the way.

Homecare Nigeria offers comprehensive, personalized care services for adults with physical disabilities. Our team of experienced caregivers is dedicated to helping clients maintain their independence while staying close to loved ones and remaining active in their communities.

Contact us today to learn more about our physical disability care services and how we can assist you or your loved one.

What is a Physical Disability?

A physical disability refers to a significant and lasting condition that affects a person’s body, limiting their ability to move, endure, or perform tasks requiring dexterity. Each physical disability is unique, impacting individuals in different ways. This can lead to challenges in performing everyday activities such as walking, sitting, standing, and controlling muscle movements.

The Importance of Home Support for Physical Disabilities

Receiving care at home allows individuals with physical disabilities to maintain social connections and stay close to loved ones, rather than relocating to a care facility. At Homecare Nigeria, we focus on each person’s strengths and abilities, helping them reach their personal goals and lead a fulfilling life. Our caregivers provide personalized support for daily activities, offering both physical assistance and companionship.

Our Services for Adults with Physical Disabilities Include:

  • Assisting with work, education, and daily responsibilities.
  • Helping you stay connected with loved ones and participate in social activities.
  • Supporting physical health by attending medical appointments, eating healthy meals, and engaging in physical activity.
  • Assisting with grocery shopping, bill payments, and other tasks that maintain independence.
  • Helping with morning and evening routines such as grooming, tidying up, and personal care.
  • Keeping your home clean with help in domestic chores such as laundry and ironing.

Whether you need weekly visits or 24-hour care, our team is here to offer the support you need. Our carers are trained to meet a wide variety of complex care needs, including physical disabilities, communication needs, and sensory impairments.
