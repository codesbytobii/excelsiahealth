    Careers and Training

    At Homecare Nigeria, we believe that the strength of our care services comes from our dedicated and compassionate team. We are always looking for caring, skilled, and passionate individuals to join our growing team of professional carers. Whether you are an experienced caregiver or just beginning your journey in the care industry, we offer a supportive environment where you can grow, develop, and make a real difference in people's lives.

    Why Join Us?

    Homecare Nigeria is not just a place to work; it’s a place where you can build a rewarding career that brings fulfilment and purpose. As part of our team, you will have the opportunity to positively impact the lives of individuals in need, while working in a nurturing and professional environment.

    • Make a Real Difference: Our carers provide essential support to individuals with complex health conditions, chronic illnesses, disabilities, and more. As part of our team, you will play a vital role in helping our clients maintain their independence and quality of life.
    • Competitive Salary and Benefits: We offer competitive pay that reflects the quality of service we provide. Our team members are well-compensated for their hard work and dedication. In addition to your salary, you’ll receive flexible working hours, performance bonuses, and paid leave.
    • Flexible Working Hours: We understand the importance of a work-life balance. Our carers have the flexibility to choose from various shifts, including full-time, part-time, and live-in care roles, allowing you to manage your personal commitments while making a meaningful contribution.
    • Career Progression: At Homecare Nigeria, we believe in investing in our team’s growth. We offer plenty of opportunities for career progression and development pathways that allow you to advance within the organisation. Whether you're interested in moving into leadership roles or gaining specialist expertise, there’s room to grow.
    • Supportive Team Environment: Our carers are part of a supportive and inclusive community. We foster a collaborative work culture, ensuring that you have the support you need to thrive in your role. You will be part of a compassionate team that values your input and encourages professional growth.

    Training and Development

    We are committed to the ongoing development of our staff through comprehensive training programs that equip you with the skills you need to excel in your career. Our training academy ensures that our carers are not only prepared to meet the needs of our clients but are also able to develop their careers over time.

    • Comprehensive Induction Programme: Every new employee undergoes a detailed induction to ensure that they are familiar with our care standards and practices. This initial training covers everything from understanding different medical conditions to delivering compassionate care that meets the unique needs of each client.
    • Continuous Professional Development (CPD): We offer a wide range of Continuous Professional Development (CPD) courses to help you stay updated with the latest advancements in care. These courses cover key areas like:
      • First aid and emergency care
      • Medication management
      • Condition-led care (e.g., dementia, Parkinson's, diabetes care)
      • Palliative and end-of-life care
      • Mental health support
      • Safeguarding and infection control
    • Specialist Training: For those interested in more advanced care roles, we provide specialised training in areas such as:
      • Nephrostomy and stoma care
      • Cancer care and oncology support
      • Post-surgical rehabilitation
      • Neurological conditions management
    • Leadership and Management Development: For carers looking to advance into supervisory or managerial roles, we offer leadership and management development programmes. These programs equip you with the skills to lead care teams, manage client relationships, and handle administrative tasks related to care provision.
    • On-the-Job Mentorship: You will have access to experienced mentors who will provide on-the-job guidance and support as you navigate your new role. Our experienced team members are always available to answer questions and provide assistance.

    Career Opportunities

    We offer a variety of career opportunities across different levels, from entry-level caregivers to specialised roles. Some of the roles we are actively recruiting for include:

    • Care Assistants (Part-time and Full-time)
    • Live-in Carers
    • Specialist Carers (e.g., dementia, cancer, neurological care)
    • Care Coordinators
    • Nursing Professionals
    • Care Team Leaders
    • Training and Development Officers

    Each role comes with its own set of responsibilities and rewards, and we are happy to work with you to find the position that best suits your skills and aspirations.

    Join Our Team Today!

    Are you ready to make a difference in the lives of others? Whether you're an experienced carer or new to the field, we would love to hear from you. Homecare Nigeria offers a nurturing environment, ongoing training, and rewarding career paths that can help you grow and thrive in the care industry.

    How to Apply

    1. Submit Your Application: Visit our Careers Page on our website and fill out the application form. Attach your CV and cover letter outlining your experience and why you’d like to join Homecare Nigeria.
    2. Interview Process: Once we review your application, successful candidates will be invited for an interview where we will discuss your skills, experience, and fit for our team.
    3. Induction and Training: If successful, you’ll go through our induction program and begin your journey with Homecare Nigeria.

    Start your fulfilling career with us today!

    For more information on available job opportunities or to inquire about training, please contact us at careers@excelsia.health. We're excited to welcome you to the Homecare Nigeria family!
