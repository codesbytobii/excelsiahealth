About Excelsia HealthTravels?

At Excelsia Health Travels, we specialize in offering safe and seamless birth tourism and medical tourism experiences, helping you access world-class healthcare abroad. Whether you're looking to give your child the advantage of birthright citizenship or seeking high-quality medical treatments at top international hospitals, we are here to guide you every step of the way.
As Nigeria’s leading provider, we ensure your journey is worry-free, handling everything from visa assistance and hospital coordination to travel and accommodation, allowing you to focus on your health and your family’s future.

Our vision is to be Nigeria’s leading home care service, empowering individuals to maintain their independence while receiving top-notch care in the comfort of their homes.

We aim to set new standards in domiciliary care services in Nigeria, ensuring that every client’s needs are met with personalised, compassionate care.

At Homecare Nigeria, our mission is simple: to improve the quality of life for our clients by delivering personalised, reliable, and professional home care services.

We understand that each person is unique, which is why we tailor our services to match individual needs, whether it's personal care, companionship, or more complex medical care.
