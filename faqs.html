    • FAQs
    • 1. What is medical tourism, and how can Excelsia Health Travels help me?

      Medical tourism involves traveling to another country to receive medical care, often at a lower cost or with quicker access to treatments. Excelsia Health Travels assists clients from Nigeria and across Africa by facilitating the entire medical travel process, from finding the right doctors and hospitals to organizing travel, accommodation, and follow-up care. Additionally, we specialize in birth tourism, helping expectant mothers travel to give birth in countries that offer citizenship to babies born there.

    • 2. What is birth tourism, and how can Excelsia Health Travels assist with it?

      Birth tourism refers to traveling to a foreign country to give birth, allowing the child to receive citizenship from that country. Excelsia Health Travels helps expecting parents with every aspect of birth tourism, from selecting the best destination and hospital to assisting with visas, accommodation, and postnatal care, ensuring a smooth, worry-free process for both mother and baby.

    • 3. What types of medical treatments and birth services does Excelsia Health Travels facilitate?

      We facilitate a wide range of medical treatments such as:

      • Cosmetic surgery
      • Cardiac care (heart surgeries)
      • Orthopaedic procedures (hip/knee replacements)
      • Fertility treatments (IVF)
      • Oncology (cancer treatments)
      • Dental care
      • Neurology (brain and spinal surgeries)
      • Organ transplants
      • Bariatric surgery (weight loss surgeries)

      For birth tourism, we assist with prenatal care, delivery, postnatal care, and obtaining birth certificates and citizenship documentation for your newborn.

    • 4. Which countries does Excelsia Health Travels partner with for medical and birth tourism?

      We partner with accredited hospitals in leading medical and birth tourism destinations, including:

      • United States
      • Canada
      • United Arab Emirates (Dubai and Abu Dhabi)
      • India
      • Turkey
      • Germany
      • United Kingdom
      • South Africa
      • Malaysia
      • Brazil
      • Singapore
      • Thailand

      These countries offer high-quality healthcare, and some also provide birthright citizenship to babies born within their borders.

    • 5. How does Excelsia Health Travels choose hospitals and doctors?

      We work with internationally accredited hospitals and experienced specialists. Our selection process focuses on the hospital’s international certifications (e.g., JCI accreditation), reputation, quality of care, patient outcomes, and the expertise of the medical staff to ensure you receive the best possible care.

    • 6. Do you assist with obtaining a visa for medical or birth tourism?

      Yes, we provide comprehensive visa assistance. Whether you’re traveling for medical treatment or birth tourism, we help gather the required documentation, including medical invitation letters, hospital appointment confirmations, and travel details, ensuring a smooth visa application process.

    • 7. How do I start the process with Excelsia Health Travels?

      Getting started is simple. You can contact us via phone, email, or by filling out the client intake form on our website. After gathering your medical or birth-related information, we’ll provide an initial consultation to discuss your treatment options, destinations, and travel arrangements.

    • 8. Can I get a cost estimate before I travel?

      Yes, after we receive your medical records or birth tourism requirements, we will provide a detailed cost estimate. This will include treatment costs, hospital stay, travel, accommodation, and any other additional services. We ensure complete transparency in pricing to avoid hidden fees.

    • 9. How long does the medical or birth tourism process take?

      The timeline depends on the treatment or birth needs, destination, and visa processing. Typically, medical or birth tourism arrangements can be made within 2-4 weeks, but we can expedite the process for urgent cases.

    • 10. Can I bring a companion during my medical or birth tourism trip?

      Yes, we can arrange travel and accommodation for companions or caregivers who wish to accompany you. For birth tourism, we can also organize family-friendly accommodations and services for your spouse, children, or other family members.

    • 11. What happens after my treatment or delivery is completed?

      For medical tourism, we arrange follow-up care with your local doctor in Nigeria and can facilitate remote consultations with your overseas doctor if necessary. For birth tourism, we help you complete the necessary documentation, including obtaining birth certificates, passports, and any citizenship paperwork for your newborn. We also ensure you receive the postnatal care and support you need before returning home.

    • 12. How do I pay for treatment, delivery, and services?

      You can make payments directly to the hospital or clinic for medical treatments or delivery services. For additional services such as travel arrangements, we offer multiple payment options, including local Nigerian payment gateways and international payment methods, ensuring a secure and easy payment process.

    • 13. Do I need health insurance for my medical or birth tourism trip?

      While health insurance is not mandatory, we recommend checking with your insurance provider to see if they cover treatments or birth-related expenses abroad. If not, we will work with you to ensure full cost transparency and help you find affordable options.

    • 14. How does Excelsia Health Travels ensure the safety and quality of care abroad?

      We only work with internationally accredited hospitals that follow global best practices in healthcare. These facilities undergo strict accreditation processes (e.g., JCI, NABH) and maintain high standards for hygiene, patient safety, and clinical care.

    • 15. What if there are complications after my treatment or delivery abroad?

      In the rare event of complications, we will coordinate with your treating physician abroad and your local healthcare provider in Nigeria. For both medical and birth tourism, we ensure continuous follow-up care and guidance to support your recovery or postpartum needs.

    • 16. Can Excelsia Health Travels help with leisure activities during my medical or birth tourism trip?

      Yes, we offer concierge services that include arranging leisure activities, sightseeing tours, and dining experiences during your stay. Whether it’s a cultural excursion or relaxation activities, we can make your medical or birth tourism trip enjoyable for you and your companions.
