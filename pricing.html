Medical and Birth Tourism Packages

At Excelsia Health Travels, we offer a variety of customizable packages designed to meet your unique medical and birth tourism needs. Whether you’re seeking essential support, enhanced services, or a premium all-inclusive experience, our packages ensure transparency, flexibility, and world-class care. Below is an overview of our packages and add-ons to help you choose the best option for your journey.

1. Standard Health Check-Up Package

Price: To Be Confirmed (TBC) with Client

This package is ideal for individuals seeking comprehensive health screenings and diagnostic services at top international hospitals. It also includes prenatal screening for maternity cases.

What’s Included:

  • Full-body health assessments.
  • Diagnostic tests tailored to your medical history and needs.
  • Prenatal screenings for expectant mothers.
  • Access to internationally accredited hospitals with state-of-the-art facilities.
2. Wellness and Recovery Package

Price: To Be Confirmed (TBC) with Client

This package combines medical treatments or maternity care with holistic wellness programs to support your recovery and overall well-being.

What’s Included:

  • Spa services, including therapeutic massages and relaxation therapies.
  • Rehabilitation programs tailored to your medical or maternity recovery needs.
  • Access to wellness retreats and holistic therapies, such as yoga and meditation.
  • Personalized care plans to ensure a smooth recovery process.
3. Custom Medical and Birth Tourism Package

Price: Flexible, Based on Client’s Needs

This package is tailored to meet the unique needs of every patient or expecting parent. It offers flexibility to accommodate different preferences, medical requirements, and maternity care needs.

What’s Included:

  • Medical or Maternity Care Needs: Tailored plans based on the type of medical procedure or maternity care required.
  • Destination and Hospital Selection: Assistance in choosing the best destination and hospital or maternity center to meet your needs.
  • Length of Stay: Customized arrangements for recovery periods, postnatal care, or extended stays.
  • Personalized Preferences: Options for travel, accommodation, and post-treatment or postnatal services to suit your lifestyle and comfort.
4. Basic Plan: Essential Support for Your Medical or Birth Journey

Price Range: ₦1,000,000 - ₦2,000,000

This plan is perfect for patients or expecting parents seeking essential support at an affordable rate. It covers all the basic services needed to ensure a smooth and stress-free experience.

What’s Included:

  • Initial Consultation and Treatment/Birth Plan:
    • Free consultation to assess your medical or birth needs.
    • Standard hospital and doctor/maternity specialist selection based on your requirements.
  • Visa Assistance: Support with visa application and documentation for medical treatment or birth tourism.
  • Flight Booking Assistance: Economy class options only.
  • Standard Accommodation (Booking): 3-star hotels or budget-friendly accommodation near the hospital or maternity center.
  • Medical or Maternity Records Transfer: Coordination of your medical records or prenatal care documents between Nigerian and international providers.
  • Pre-Treatment/Pre-Birth Appointment Scheduling: Assistance with scheduling hospital or maternity appointments, prenatal check-ups, and consultations.
  • Post-Treatment/Post-Delivery Follow-Up: Basic telemedicine consultations with your healthcare provider after treatment or delivery.
  • 24/7 Phone Support: Continuous support throughout your journey to ensure peace of mind.
5. Silver Plan: Enhanced Services with Personalized Assistance

Price Range: ₦2,000,000 - ₦4,000,000

This plan is ideal for those looking for comprehensive services and personalized assistance, ensuring the highest standard of care for both medical treatment and childbirth.

What’s Included:

  • Initial Consultation and Treatment/Birth Plan:
    • Free consultation with comprehensive hospital and specialist selection.
    • Multiple options for accredited hospitals, doctors, or maternity centers.
  • Visa Assistance & Expedited Processing: Full visa support with expedited processing for urgent cases.
  • Flight Booking Assistance: Economy and Business class options, with flexible booking based on your medical or maternity needs.
  • Accommodation Arrangements (Booking): 3–4-star hotels tailored to your preferences, close to the medical or maternity facility.
  • Medical/Maternity Records Transfer & Coordination: Secure transfer and coordination of medical or maternity records between local and international providers.
  • Pre-Treatment/Pre-Birth and Post-Treatment/Post-Delivery Scheduling: Coordination of all appointments, including prenatal check-ups, tests, follow-up consultations, and postnatal care.
  • On-Site Support: Local representative at your destination to assist with hospital coordination, language support, and local logistics.
  • Post-Treatment/Post-Delivery Follow-Up: Telemedicine consultations and local rehabilitation or postnatal care arrangements.
  • Health Check-Up Packages (Optional): Comprehensive diagnostic screenings or prenatal assessments.
  • 24/7 Phone & Email Support: Ongoing support via phone and email throughout your journey.
6. Gold Plan: Premium, All-Inclusive Medical and Birth Tourism Experience

Price Range: ₦4,000,000+

This plan is designed for patients and expecting mothers seeking the highest level of service. It offers a premium, all-inclusive package with luxury travel arrangements and comprehensive support throughout your medical treatment or birth experience.

What’s Included:

  • Personalized Consultation & VIP Treatment Plan: A fully customized plan based on your medical or maternity needs, with access to top-tier healthcare providers.
  • Premium Hospital & Doctor/Maternity Specialist Selection: Exclusive access to internationally accredited hospitals and specialists in the best medical and maternity centers.
  • Visa Assistance with Fast-Track Approval: Fast-tracked visa application and approval process for urgent medical treatments or births.
  • Flight Booking Assistance: Premium Business Class and First-Class flight options with additional flexibility for rescheduling.
  • Premium Accommodation Arrangements: Luxury stays in 4–5-star hotels or serviced apartments near the medical or maternity facility, offering comfort and privacy.
  • Private Local Transportation: Chauffeur-driven transport, including airport transfers and daily shuttles to the hospital or maternity facility.
  • Pre-Treatment/Pre-Birth & Post-Treatment/Post-Delivery Concierge Services: Full coordination of all appointments, treatments, delivery arrangements, and follow-ups.
  • On-Site Premium Support: A dedicated personal assistant available throughout your stay to handle logistics, translation services, and any other requests.
  • Post-Treatment/Post-Delivery Recovery: Access to local or international rehabilitation services, extended postnatal care, and wellness programs for recovery.
  • Wellness & Recovery Packages: Includes access to wellness retreats, spa treatments, and holistic therapies.
  • Second Medical Opinion Services: Optional access to a second opinion from top global healthcare experts.
  • Emergency Medical Assistance: Fast-track visa and travel support for urgent medical treatments or births.
  • 24/7 VIP Support: Priority phone, email, and personal support throughout your journey.

Add-Ons (Available for All Packages)

  • Health Insurance Assistance: Price upon request; we liaise with your insurance provider to facilitate coverage for medical treatments or maternity care abroad.
  • Extended Stay Accommodation: Price upon request for additional accommodation during long-term treatments or extended postnatal recovery.
  • Companion Travel Arrangements: Price upon request to cover accommodation and transport for family members accompanying you.
  • Premium Wellness Program: Price upon request for wellness and recovery services, including yoga, detox programs, therapeutic massages, and holistic treatments.

Payment Terms and Financing Options

  • Upfront Payment: A 50% deposit is required before initiating services, with the remaining 50% payable before departure.
  • Flexible Payment Plans: We offer payment plans for major treatments, surgeries, and maternity packages, allowing you to spread the cost over an agreed period.
  • Insurance Liaison: We work with your insurance provider to ensure that any reimbursable treatments or maternity costs are properly coordinated.
