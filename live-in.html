Experience Round-the-Clock Care and Companionship

At Homecare Nigeria, we specialize in providing live-in care services that deliver continuous support and companionship without the need for relocation. Personalised Live-In Care for Independence

Our live-in care is designed to ensure you maintain your independence while enjoying the comfort and familiarity of your own home. We work closely with you to develop a customised care plan that addresses your unique needs, ensuring the support you receive is exactly what you require to lead a fulfilling life.

Receive the care you need in the place you love. Learn more about our live-in care services.

What is Live-In Care?

Live-in care is a popular option for individuals who prefer to remain in their homes while receiving full-time, professional care. A dedicated caregiver will live with you, offering 24-hour support for all your personal and healthcare needs. Our goal is to empower you to live independently, offering personalised support that encourages autonomy.

Unlike other forms of home care, live-in care ensures a caregiver is available round-the-clock, providing continuous, reliable support.

Our Live-In Care Services Include:

  • Personal Care: Assistance with daily activities such as bathing, toileting, and dressing.
  • Meal Preparation: Helping you prepare nutritious, flavourful meals that meet your dietary needs.
  • Household Chores: Support with tasks like laundry, general cleaning, and maintaining a tidy home.
  • Social Outings: Accompanying you to the grocery store, visits with friends and family, or religious services.
  • Specialised Healthcare: Expert care for catheter, stoma, and PEG needs.
  • Companionship: Offering emotional support and companionship throughout the day.
  • Night Care: Providing assistance throughout the night to ensure your safety and comfort.

At Homecare Nigeria, we are committed to promoting independence while delivering high-quality care. Our live-in care services enable you to live life on your own terms.

Benefits of Live-In Care

Choosing live-in care offers several advantages, both physical and emotional:

  • Stay at Home: You don’t need to leave or sell your home to receive the care you need.
  • Stay Connected: Remain in your local community, surrounded by friends and family.
  • Avoid Separation: Keep married couples together by providing support for both partners at home.
  • Maintain Independence: Continue to enjoy your favourite activities and maintain control over your daily life, with support whenever needed.

Live-in care in Nigeria allows individuals to maintain independence while receiving personalised care services that address their specific needs. Get in touch with us today to explore the benefits of live-in care and how it can help you or your loved one.
