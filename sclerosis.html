    Multiple Sclerosis (MS) Care Services in Nigeria

    With over 10,000 people in Nigeria affected by Multiple Sclerosis (MS), it is one of the most common conditions impacting the central nervous system. Discovering you have MS can be overwhelming, with feelings of isolation, fear, or frustration. At Homecare Nigeria, we deeply understand the profound effects an MS diagnosis can have on your life, and we’re here to support you in managing the condition and adapting to the necessary lifestyle changes.

    We offer specialised care services for individuals living with MS, providing customised care plans to meet your unique needs. Our comprehensive support extends to both you and your loved ones, ensuring that you receive the assistance and care needed to navigate the challenges of this condition.

    What is Multiple Sclerosis?

    Multiple Sclerosis (MS) is a chronic condition affecting the brain and spinal cord. It can cause problems with mobility, vision, balance, and other essential functions. As an autoimmune disease, MS leads the body’s immune system to attack the protective myelin sheath covering nerve fibres, disrupting communication between the brain and the rest of the body.

    Recognising Common Symptoms of MS

    The symptoms of MS can vary depending on the specific nerves involved and the severity of the damage. Common symptoms include:

    • Tiredness and fatigue.
    • Light-headedness or dizziness.
    • Unsteadiness or balance issues.
    • Muscle spasms and weakness.
    • Numbness or tingling sensations.
    • Difficulty swallowing.
    • Challenges with limb mobility.
    • Bowel and bladder dysfunction.
    • Vision problems, including double vision or vision loss.
    • Memory and cognitive challenges.
    • Sexual dysfunction.

    Symptoms of MS can be unpredictable, with some individuals experiencing symptoms intermittently, while others may go through long periods of remission. As the disease progresses, symptoms often worsen due to nerve damage. Treatment focuses on slowing disease progression and managing symptoms through medications, therapies, and lifestyle adjustments.

    Personalised Care for MS at Home

    Living with MS can be challenging, but at Homecare Nigeria, we are committed to providing compassionate support to help manage daily symptoms and enhance your overall well-being. Our team of professionals will work closely with you to develop a personalised MS care plan that meets your specific needs, allowing you to live comfortably in your own home.

    Our MS Care Services Include:

    • Medication Management: Overseeing medication schedules to ensure proper administration.
    • Personal Care: Assisting with bathing, dressing, and daily grooming tasks.
    • Continence Support: Providing assistance with toileting needs, including catheter and continence management.
    • Exercise Assistance: Developing a consistent exercise routine to improve mobility and boost mood.
    • Dietary Support: Ensuring nutritional needs are met to promote overall health.
    • Household Help: Assisting with household chores to ease daily burdens.
