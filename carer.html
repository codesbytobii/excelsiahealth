    Step-by-Step Guide to Finding a Carer

    • Step 1: Access the List of Carers
      1. Visit the designated page on our website where the list of carers is available.
      2. Scroll through the page to view the available carers and their profiles.
    • Step 2: Filter Carers Based on Your Preferences
      1. Use the filtering options provided on the page to narrow down your choices.
        • Filters may include criteria such as location, experience level, specialized skills, availability, or language preferences.
      2. Select the filters that best match your needs. For example, if you need a carer with experience in elderly care, select that option.
      3. Once you apply the filters, the list will update to show only the carers that meet your preferences.
    • Step 3: Select a Carer and Proceed to the Sign-Up Page
      1. Review the profiles of the filtered carers to find the one that best suits your needs.
        • Each profile may include details such as the carer's qualifications, experience, and a brief introduction.
      2. Once you identify your preferred carer, click on their profile or the "Select" button.
      3. You will be redirected to a Sign-Up Page.
        • On this page, you will need to provide your contact information and additional details about your care requirements.
    • Step 4: Follow Up and Team Contact
      1. After completing the sign-up process, our team will receive your request and match it with the selected carer.
      2. A member of our team will review your submission and get in touch with you to confirm the details.
      3. You may also receive an email or phone call to discuss your needs further and finalize the arrangement.
    • Additional Notes
      • If you encounter any issues during the process, Contact Us section on the page for assistance.
      • Ensure that the information you provide during sign-up is accurate to avoid delays in the follow-up process.
