Introduction

Excelsia Health offers medical tourism and birth tourism facilitation services for clients seeking medical treatment or maternity care abroad. We act as a coordinator between clients and international healthcare providers, including hospitals, clinics, and specialists, ensuring a seamless healthcare experience.

Services Offered

Excelsia Health provides a comprehensive range of services, including but not limited to:

  • Medical consultation and advisory services for treatments and birth tourism.
  • Coordination with international hospitals, clinics, maternity centres, and specialists.
  • Visa assistance for medical and birth tourism travel.
  • Travel arrangements, including flight bookings and accommodation for medical and birth tourism clients.
  • Transportation services to and from hospitals, maternity centres, and airports.
  • Post-treatment and post-delivery follow-up care and coordination.
  • Insurance support and claims assistance.

Our services are strictly facilitation services. Excelsia Health is not a healthcare provider and does not directly provide medical treatment, maternity care, or medical advice. All medical and maternity decisions are made between you and the healthcare providers in the destination country.

Client Responsibilities

As a client of Excelsia Health, you agree to the following responsibilities:

  • Accurate Information: You must provide complete, accurate, and up-to-date medical records, maternity history, and other relevant details for your treatment or birth tourism journey.
  • Medical/Maternity Decisions: All medical and maternity-related decisions are made by licensed healthcare providers in the destination country. Excelsia Health will not be liable for any medical advice, treatments, or delivery outcomes.
  • Compliance with Local Laws: You agree to comply with the laws and regulations of the destination country, including visa requirements, local healthcare protocols, and customs.
  • Payments: You are responsible for paying all agreed fees, including medical/maternity fees, travel costs, and service fees, in a timely manner.

Payment Policy

Excelsia Health requires full or partial payment for services rendered, depending on the nature of the service, as follows:

  • Deposit: A non-refundable deposit may be required to confirm your booking with a medical or maternity provider or to initiate visa assistance.
  • Full Payment: The full cost of medical treatments, maternity services, travel, and accommodation must be paid in advance, based on the payment schedule provided by Excelsia Health.
  • Currency: Payments can be made in Nigerian Naira (NGN) or other accepted international currencies. Payment methods include bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and supported online payment gateways.
  • Refunds: Refunds will only be considered under exceptional circumstances, such as if the medical or maternity provider cancels a procedure before it commences. All refund requests must be made in writing and are subject to approval.

Cancellation and Refund Policy

Cancellation by the Client:

  • Cancellations made more than 14 days before the scheduled travel or treatment may be eligible for a partial refund, excluding non-refundable deposits and administrative fees.
  • Cancellations made less than 14 days before the scheduled treatment or travel will not be eligible for a refund.

Cancellation by Excelsia Health:

Excelsia Health reserves the right to cancel or reschedule bookings due to unforeseen circumstances such as travel restrictions, medical or maternity provider issues, or force majeure events. In such cases, we will work to reschedule the services or offer an appropriate refund.

Travel and Visa Assistance

Excelsia Health will assist with the visa application process and travel arrangements but cannot guarantee visa approvals or travel arrangements. Clients are responsible for ensuring they have the correct visas and travel documents.

  • Visa Application: We provide visa invitation letters and required documentation for medical and birth tourism travel. However, the final decision rests with the embassy or consulate.
  • Travel Arrangements: We assist in arranging flights, accommodation, and ground transportation based on your preferences. However, clients are responsible for ensuring all travel details are accurate and comply with the laws of the destination country.
