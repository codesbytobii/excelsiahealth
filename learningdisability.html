Learning Disabilities Support Services in Nigeria

Finding fulfilment and significance in life is a universal desire. People with learning disabilities are also included.

At Homecare Nigeria, we specialise in providing personalised care for adults with learning disabilities, empowering them to lead independent and fulfilling lives. Our compassionate approach is tailored to meet each individual’s unique needs, ensuring they can thrive and live self-directed lives. We work closely with families and individuals to develop a personalised support plan that prioritises their goals, needs, and preferences.

Providing Care for Individuals with Learning Disabilities at Home

Caring for a loved one with a learning disability can sometimes feel overwhelming for family members and carers. Homecare Nigeria is here to provide the guidance and support needed to manage your loved one’s condition. Our team is committed to offering not only physical assistance but also fostering emotional and social development. Our carers provide compassionate companionship, listening to your loved one's concerns and supporting them in their interests.

Supporting individuals with learning disabilities at home allows them greater autonomy and freedom to make decisions that might not be possible in structured environments like residential care. This form of supported living enhances well-being by maintaining close connections with family and friends in a familiar setting.

Contact us today to learn more about our learning disabilities support services and how we can help your family in the comfort of your own home.

What is a Learning Disability?

A learning disability refers to a reduced intellectual ability and difficulty in performing everyday tasks. The level of support needed varies, from mild assistance with community integration or employment to round-the-clock care for more severe or profound disabilities.

In Nigeria, over 32 million individuals live with a learning disability, each impacted differently. Many rely on loved ones for assistance, but with the help of a dedicated learning disability carer, individuals and families can better manage daily life.

Supported Living for Adults with Learning Disabilities

At Homecare Nigeria, we focus on providing exceptional support that promotes independence, effective communication, and personal growth. Our services help individuals with learning disabilities live the life they desire, filled with enriching and fulfilling experiences. We recognise and nurture the unique strengths and abilities of each individual, offering a wide range of personalised care options.

Our Learning Disabilities Support Services Include:

  • Assisting with daily responsibilities such as work, education, and social activities.
  • Helping individuals stay connected with loved ones and engage in hobbies and social events.
  • Supporting physical health through regular medical check-ups, nutritious meals, and physical activity.
  • Providing help with grocery shopping, bill payments, and other tasks that maintain independence.
  • Assisting with daily routines, including morning and evening personal care tasks.
  • Helping to keep the home clean and tidy with assistance in household chores like laundry and ironing.

Our care assistants are trained to support individuals with a variety of complex care needs, including autistic spectrum disorders, physical disabilities, and sensory impairments. Our focus is on promoting independence and empowering individuals to achieve their personal goals.
